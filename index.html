Cooking with Creativity Recipe: Spiced Vegetable Stir Fry

Here's a simple and delicious recipe that incorporates the principles of cooking with creativity. This spiced vegetable stir fry is the perfect way to showcase your love of bold flavors and unique ingredients.

Ingredients:

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil - 1 onion, chopped - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 red bell pepper, sliced - 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced - 1 eggplant, sliced - 1 teaspoon ground coriander - 1 teaspoon ground cumin - 1 teaspoon turmeric - Salt and pepper to taste - 1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Instructions:

1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. 2. Add the onion, garlic, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, and eggplant to the wok. Cook for 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender. 3. Stir in the coriander, cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2 minutes, or until fragrant. 4. Stir in the cilantro and serve hot over rice or noodles.

This spiced vegetable stir fry is a perfect example of cooking with creativity. By incorporating bold spices and unique ingredients, you can create a delicious and exciting meal that showcases your love of food.
